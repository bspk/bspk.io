About Us

Bespoke Engineering is an independent consulting company specializing in internet identity, cross-domain security, software engineering, and systems architecture. We strongly believe in the power of open standards and open source software and have many years of direct experience in both. We have deep expertise in OAuth, OpenID Connect, JOSE, and other modern web security standards, and we have broad experience in deploying these technologies in a variety of environments. We offer a variety of custom consulting services, including commercial support and customization of the MITREid Connect open source project.
